"Motivatr"

Class: Human Computer InteractionAug. – Dec. 2017

As a member of a three–person team, we prototyped the design for a mobile application design intended to help users curb bad procrastination habits.

The design process consisted of the following stages:

  • User Research
    • 10 participants answered
      • 17 long answer interview questions
      • 33 multiple choice survey questions

Includes wireframes, hi- and low-fidelity prototypes, and results of user testing and research througout.
